Beaver

Rodent

The beaver is a large, primarily nocturnal semiaquatic rodent. Castor includes two extant species, the North American beaver and Eurasian beaver. Beavers are know for building dams, canals, and lodges.

Scientific name: Castor

Higher classification: Castoridae

Rank: Genus

Mass: Eurasian beaver 11-30kg, North American beaver: 11-32kg

Length: Eurasian beaver 80-100cm, North American beaver: 74-90cm

Mass: Eurasian beaver 107 days, North American beaver: 128 days
